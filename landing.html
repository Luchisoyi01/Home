Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about living at Student Haven.

How do I apply for accommodation?
You can apply through our online portal or contact our admissions team directly. We'll guide you through availability, room selection, and the booking process.
What is included in the rent?
Rent includes utilities (water, electricity), high-speed Wi-Fi, access to all common facilities, security, and basic maintenance. Meal plans are available separately.
Can I request a room change?
Room changes are possible subject to availability. Please submit a request through the student portal or speak with our accommodation manager.
Are meals provided?
We offer flexible cafeteria meal plans that can be added to your accommodation package. Various dietary requirements can be accommodated.
What is the guest policy?
Guests are welcome during designated hours and must be registered at reception. Overnight stays require prior approval and are limited to 3 nights per month.
Is there parking available?
Limited parking spaces are available for residents at an additional monthly fee. We encourage use of public transport and provide secure bicycle storage.
